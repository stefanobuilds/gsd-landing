The Science

Why this format actually works.

👭

Body Doubling

Working alongside others boosts focus and motivation through social presence and mirror neurons.

💪

Implementation Intentions

Setting clear plans for what you'll work on dramatically increases follow-through on your goals.

👀

Accountability

Sharing your goals with others and setting commitments raises success rates dramatically.

🚀

Optimal Sprint Length

~52 minute sprints match your brain's peak focus window. Refocusing after distraction takes 23+ minutes.
